The Perpose

The perpose of this website is to get people to know more about the United States of America. We also want to show people especially US citizens that it is okay to be patriots. We also want to show and tell people about US history. We also want to show people that the US was not founded on racism but was founded by the idea of freedom. We also want to bring back the american spirit to the citizens of the United states of America and to also pass down the the american spirit and with the ideas that have kept our country free to our kids.
